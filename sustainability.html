Ecological Restoration and Net-Positive Urban Design Policy

NeoCity stands as the global hallmark of successful ecological recovery, operating under a strict **Net-Positive Carbon Policy**—the city draws down more CO₂ than its operations emit. This mandate is reflected in all urban architecture, which utilizes advanced **carbon-sequestering bio-concrete and timber composites**. Buildings are integrated into the environment, functioning as vertical habitats with mandatory **Biophilic Zoning** that manages rainwater capture and provides large-scale natural air filtration.


Energy independence is absolute. The city is powered primarily by a high-density, localized **Fusion Power Grid**, supplemented by immense kinetic wave generators and ubiquitous solar skins integrated across all exterior surfaces. All wastewater is processed through comprehensive **closed-loop bio-filtration parks** within the city perimeter, ensuring 100% water recovery and reuse, eliminating contamination of external water sources and supporting the urban farms.


The Circular Economy and Sustainable Mobility Mandate

The city's economic life is governed by the **Mandatory Circular Economy Act (MCEA)**. This policy dictates that all manufactured products must be designed with embedded digital passports to track materials and guarantee they are perpetually recyclable or perpetually repairable. The model eliminates ownership of disposable goods in favor of service and utility leases. This framework is supported by a robust public resource infrastructure that ensures raw materials are fed back into the manufacturing cycle, making "landfill" a term relegated to history.


Food production is highly localized and sustainable. Security is guaranteed by vast, optimized **Aeroponic Sky-Farms** and **Atmospheric Farming Domes** built into the superstructure, which grow genetically optimized, nutrient-rich crops using minimal water and zero pesticides. Transportation adheres to a strict **Zero-Emissions Priority Mandate**. Inter-city travel is handled by continental **Maglev Hyperloops**, while personal mobility is managed by autonomous, non-polluting **Electric Commuter Pods** and VTOL sky-taxis, which are routed to keep all heavy vehicle infrastructure permanently outside the atmospheric living space.

We are a city model of technological innovation, net-positive sustainability, and fluid, dynamic culture.
